Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FakeSteps ("the App") handles your data. FakeSteps is an open-source Android application developed by ivan-valdes.

In short: FakeSteps does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data stays on your device.

What Data the App Writes

FakeSteps writes the following types of records to Health Connect on your device:

  • Step count records (StepsRecord) -- the number of steps you configure in the app.
  • Exercise session records (ExerciseSessionRecord) -- a walking session with the duration you configure.

These records are written locally to Health Connect. The App does not read any data from Health Connect.

Data Collection

FakeSteps does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or telemetry is collected
  • No crash reports are collected
  • No device identifiers are collected
  • No location data is collected

Data Transmission

FakeSteps does not transmit any data to external servers. The App does not make any network requests. It operates entirely offline.

Data Storage

The App stores your configuration preferences (step count, walk duration, scheduled time) locally on your device using Android DataStore. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

FakeSteps does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms. The only external integration is with Health Connect, which is a system-level Android API managed by Google on your device.

Advertising

FakeSteps does not display any advertisements.

Children's Privacy

FakeSteps does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App does not collect information from anyone.

Health Connect Permissions

FakeSteps requests the following Health Connect permissions:

  • Write Steps -- to create step count records
  • Write Exercise -- to create walking exercise session records

These permissions are used solely for writing the fake step and exercise data you configure. No read permissions are requested.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Since FakeSteps is open source, all changes are also visible in the GitHub repository.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can open an issue on the GitHub repository.
