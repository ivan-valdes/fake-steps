How it works

1

Configure

Set your desired daily step count, walk duration, and the time you want the entry to be recorded.

2

Schedule

FakeSteps sets an exact alarm that fires reliably at your chosen time every day, even in Doze mode.

3

Write

When the alarm fires, a background worker writes a StepsRecord and ExerciseSessionRecord to Health Connect.

4

Repeat

The alarm automatically reschedules for the next day. If the device reboots, the schedule is restored.
